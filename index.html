About Us

REALBRAND MEDIA LIMITED is a Kenyan firm engaged in Communications that believes branding makes a company iconic, by Building relations through human relations.
We are end to end Public Relation and Media Consultancy Company with a strong background in the financial communication, corporate reputation, Public affairs, Media Relations and Crisis Management that seeks to solve recurrent problems that institutions face, with our headquarters in Westlands Area, Nairobi Kenya. Established in 2019, Realbrand Media Company is managed by a team of professionals with more than 20years of diverse experience across corporate management, Media Relations and managing financial communication solutions.
